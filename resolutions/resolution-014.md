# RESOLUTIONS OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Resolution in writing of the board of directors of Hypha Worker Co-operative Inc. (the "Co-operative") held on this 8th day of December, 2025.

## BACKGROUND
1. The Board of Directors of Hypha Worker Co-operative deems it necessary and appropriate to designate an individual with authority to execute documents and conduct transactions on behalf of the Co-operative.

2. Amanda Argast (also known as Andi Argast) has been identified as a suitable individual to be granted such signing authority.

## IT WAS RESOLVED THAT
1. Amanda Argast is hereby authorized to sign contracts, agreements, and other documents on behalf of Hypha Worker Co-operative Inc.

2. This signing authority shall remain in effect until modified or revoked by subsequent resolution of the Board of Directors.

3. Financial institutions, vendors, and other third parties may rely upon this resolution as evidence of Amanda Argast's authority to act on behalf of the Co-operative.

4. The Secretary of the Board is directed to provide certified copies of this resolution to any party that may reasonably require it.
