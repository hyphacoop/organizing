# **RESOLUTIONS OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS**

Resolution in writing of the board of directors of Hypha Worker Co-operative Inc. (the "Co-operative") held on this 11th day of February, 2026\.

## BACKGROUND

The Governance WG has proposed a set of conditional allocation goals to govern the special allocation of surplus in FY7.

## IT WAS RESOLVED THAT

The Board approves of the following goals for the 2026 conditional allocation program:

- Be profitable this fiscal year  
  - Total revenue minus operating expenses  
  - Target revenue does not count portfolio growth (e.g. crypto gain/loss, share appreciation)  
- Maintain between 10-15 member-track staff  
- Scale up at least two new or existing small clients into the $100k range, at least one with possibility to graduate into Stability in the future  
- Land one non-crypto client (can be one of the two above contracts)
